Breeze OS Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

Breeze OS ("we", "us") provides accounting and property-management software for owners of residential rental property. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and the third-party service providers we share it with.

Information we collect

  • Account information — name, email, organization name, and authentication credentials supplied when you create or sign into an account.
  • Property and tenant records — addresses, lease terms, contact details for tenants and vendors, that you enter or import from your prior property-management system.
  • Financial data — chart of accounts, journal entries, bank transactions retrieved via Plaid, and other bookkeeping records you create or sync into the platform.

How we use your information

  • Operate the application: accounting, reporting, reconciliation.
  • Improve and secure the service: error monitoring, abuse prevention.
  • Send service notifications (e.g. authentication required for a linked bank). We do not use your data for advertising.

Third-party service providers

We share information with the following service providers only to the extent necessary to deliver the service:

  • Clerk Inc. — Handles user authentication. Account email, name, and (if applicable) social-provider identifiers (Google, Apple, etc.) are stored with Clerk under their own privacy policy (clerk.com/legal/privacy). Passwords are never stored in Breeze OS's database; Clerk manages them.
  • Plaid Inc. — We use Plaid to connect to your financial institutions, retrieve account information, and pull transaction history. When you link an account, Plaid collects your bank credentials directly (we never see them) and provides us with an access token plus the account, balance, and transaction data you have authorized. Plaid's handling of this data is governed by Plaid's own privacy policy (plaid.com/legal). You may revoke Plaid's access at any time via your Plaid Portal or by removing the linked account in Breeze OS.
  • Anthropic, PBC — We send transaction descriptions and your one-line categorization hints to Anthropic's Claude API so the platform can generate reconciliation rules. We do not send account numbers, routing numbers, or personally identifying tenant data.
  • Vercel Inc. — Hosts the application and its database in the United States.
  • AppFolio — If you choose to import data from AppFolio, we authenticate to AppFolio with credentials you provide and pull the specific property records you authorize.

Data storage and security

  • Financial access tokens (Plaid access tokens, AppFolio credentials) are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM with a per-deployment encryption key.
  • All traffic between your browser, our servers, and our service providers is encrypted in transit via TLS 1.2+.
  • Database backups follow our hosting provider's standard retention schedule.

Data retention

We retain financial records for as long as your account is active plus the period required to satisfy your accounting, tax, and audit obligations (typically seven years). You may request export of your data, or deletion of all non-required records, by emailing the address below.

Your rights

  • Access, correct, or export the data we hold about you.
  • Revoke Plaid's access to your financial institutions at any time.
  • Delete your account; financial records may be retained as required by law.

Children's privacy

Breeze OS is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when we revise this policy. Material changes will be communicated via email or an in-app notice.
